Pump House

The Pump House is a thriving arts centre in the Lower High Street area of Watford that has been providing high quality artistic events for over 45 years and is a hub for emerging talent.

Watford Museum

Open from 10am to 5pm, Thursday to Saturday, the Visit England accredited museum is housed in the former offices of Benskins Brewery. Telling the story of Watford past and present, the museum has displays of local history, industry and archaeology.

Watford Palace Theater

Everyone’s welcome at the Palace. The theatre aim to inspire and entertain through inventive, ambitious and inclusive drama, new plays, musicals, dance and family shows; free outdoor festivals; diverse stand-up; and a much-loved traditional pantomime.

Watford Colosseum

Watford Colosseum is a 1,260 seat venue in the heart of Watford and is home to the best live music and entertainment outside London.

Bhaktivedanta Manor

Bhaktivedanta Manor, donated by former Beatles member, George Harrison, is a real sanctuary for the soul, with its farmlands, beautiful gardens and temple.
